QUESTION 1 [4 Marks]
A vehicle starts from rest and accelerates uniformly down a straight track 
at a rate of 4.7 m/s². Calculate the total displacement of the vehicle 
by the time its speedometer registers exactly 24 m/s.
------------------------------------------------------------------

QUESTION 2 [4 Marks]
A motorcycle starts from rest and accelerates uniformly down a straight track 
at a rate of 4.4 m/s². Calculate the total displacement of the motorcycle 
by the time its speedometer registers exactly 24 m/s.
------------------------------------------------------------------

QUESTION 3 [4 Marks]
A electric sedan starts from rest and accelerates uniformly down a straight track 
at a rate of 3.4 m/s². Calculate the total displacement of the electric sedan 
by the time its speedometer registers exactly 24 m/s.
------------------------------------------------------------------

SOLUTION FOR QUESTION 1:
1. Identify Knowns: u = 0 m/s, a = 4.7 m/s², v = 24 m/s
2. Formula Selection: v² = u² + 2as
3. Algebraic Rearrangement: s = (v² - u²) / 2a
4. Calculation:
   s = (24² - 0²) / (2 * 4.7)
   s = 576 / 9.4
   s = 61.28 meters
